About Kiese

Kiese is more than just a platform it is a cultural movement dedicated to promoting Congolese art, culture, and creatives around the world. The name "Kiese," drawn from the Kikongo language, means "joy," and it is with this same spirit of joy that we celebrate and share the richness of Congolese craftsmanship and cultural expression. Our mission is to honor local talent, preserve traditional and contemporary know-how, and amplify the voices of artisans and artists on both local and international stages.

Join Us

Our Mission

We highlight local artisans from the DRC by valuing their talent and cultural contribution.

Why Craftsmanship?

Craftsmanship is a source of employment and a pillar of Congolese cultural identity. By supporting artisans, we strengthen the local economy and ensure the transmission of know-how.

What We Offer

  • Discovery of artisanal products
  • Connection with local artisans
  • Promotion of events and craft fairs
  • Online galleries and portfolios
