Site Plan – Kiese

Site Name

Name:Kiese

"Kiese" comes from the Kikongo language and means "joy". This name reflects the positive impact of local craftsmanship on individuals and communities in the Democratic Republic of Congo. It emphasizes the pride, creativity, and happiness that come from valuing and sharing cultural talents.

Site Purpose

Craftsmanship is a key sector of the economy in the DRC, offering employment opportunities and promoting cultural heritage. This website aims to raise awareness, promote, and encourage local entrepreneurship by highlighting Congolese talent.

Scenarios

  • Where can I discover traditional handmade crafts from the Democratic Republic of Congo?
  • How can I contact a featured artisan to purchase their products?
  • What are the different types of Congolese craftsmanship and their cultural significance?
  • Are there upcoming artisan fairs or exhibitions in the region?
  • How does Kiese support local artisans and promote their work?
  • Can I submit my own craftwork to be featured on the site?
  • Is there a gallery or portfolio showcasing artisan creations?
